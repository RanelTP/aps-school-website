Extra Curricular Activites...


  • Extracurricular activities offer kids a chance to explore new interests that are not part of the regular school curriculum. They may provide more physical activity than what they would get during the school day and at home.
  • Extracurricular activities also provide fun and enjoyment, which can help keep kids motivated about school when their school work becomes challenging. Extracurricular activities help children gain new skills, learn to socialize, gain leadership ability and learn more about their own interests.

    Extra Curricular Activites are as follows :-

    Physical Activites.

    Army Public School Cannanore encompasses a diverse range of pursuits. These include traditional athletics such as track and field events, team and individual sports like football and badminton, mass drill and physical training sessions emphasizing discipline and fitness, cycling as both exercise and eco-friendly transportation, adventure sports with obstacle courses, inter-house competitions to foster healthy rivalry, and the inclusion of yoga and meditation for mental well-being. These activities collectively contribute to the holistic development of students, promoting physical fitness, teamwork, discipline, and overall wellness.

    Literary and Academic

    In Army Public School Cannanore, a variety of literary and academic activities are integral to students' intellectual development. These include the publication of a school magazine showcasing creative works, a news bulletin for information dissemination, general knowledge quizzes promoting broader learning, essay writing competitions fostering critical thinking, handwriting contests emphasizing neatness, debates and discussions enhancing communication skills, literary events celebrating language and creativity, and workshops on creative writing. These activities collectively contribute to students' intellectual growth, encouraging creativity, critical analysis, effective communication, and a lifelong passion for learning.

    Cultural Activites

    In Army Public School Cannanore, cultural activities are integral to the vibrant school environment, providing a platform for students to showcase artistic talents. These include drawing, painting, music, dance, folk songs, plays, recitation, extempore speaking, art and craft workshops, cultural festivals, talent shows, and competitions. These activities promote creativity, teamwork, self-expression, and an appreciation for diverse cultures, contributing to the holistic development of students.
