Extra Curricular Activities


Extracurricular Benefits

  • Extracurricular activities offer kids a chance to explore new interests that are not part of the regular school curriculum.
  • They provide fun and enjoyment, helping to keep kids motivated about school.
  • Activities help children gain new skills, learn to socialize, and develop leadership abilities.

Physical Activities

Army Public School Cannanore encompasses a diverse range of pursuits, including athletics, team sports, physical training, cycling, adventure sports, inter-house competitions, yoga, and meditation.

Literary and Academic Activities

These activities include the publication of a school magazine, quizzes, essay writing competitions, debates, and workshops on creative writing.

Cultural Activities

These activities provide a platform for artistic expression, including drawing, painting, music, dance, plays, and talent shows.
