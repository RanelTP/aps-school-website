Army Public School Cannanore

Army Public School Cannanore marked its epoch since 06 Jul 1985. The humble beginning was as a KG School under the Chairmanship of Col. Kharkhi, DSC Centre, Cannanore. The initial flame of knowledge lit on that day is directly carried to this day under the benevolence of the present Chairman Col Pushpendra Jhinkwan. The school was upgraded to an Upper Primary School in 1990. Army Welfare Education Society took charge of the school in 1991. Senior Secondary Status was granted to the school in 2006. Now the school has two division each at all levels with Science and Commerce stream at the Plus two level.

Discover Our Facility

  • Classrooms: The school has well-equipped classrooms with modern teaching aids and technology to enhance the learning experience for students.
  • Library: The school has a well-stocked library with a vast collection of books, periodicals, and other reading materials that cater to the needs of both students and teachers.
  • Laboratories: The school has laboratories for physics, chemistry, biology and computer science. These labs provide hands-on learning experiences for students and help them understand complex concepts in a better way.
  • Auditorium: The school has a spacious auditorium where various events, functions, and cultural programs are organized throughout the year.
  • Medical facility: The school has a medical room with medical staff to provide first aid and medical care to students in case of emergencies.
