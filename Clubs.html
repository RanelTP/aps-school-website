Discover Our Clubs...


Chess Club

The Chess Club at Army Public School Cannanore is a group of students with a shared interest in playing and improving their chess skills. It involves regular chess sessions, participation in tournaments, strategy discussions, training sessions, and the active promotion of chess culture within the school community.

Yoga Club

The Yoga Club at Army Public School Cannanore is a vibrant community of students who actively participate in practicing and enhancing their yoga skills. The club organizes regular yoga sessions, engages in in-depth discussions about yoga techniques and benefits, hosts events or workshops related to yoga, and fervently promotes a culture of wellness and mindfulness within the school community.

Karate Club

The Karate Club at Army Public School Cannanore thrives as a dynamic community where students actively engage in the pursuit of martial arts excellence. This club is a hub for enthusiasts dedicated to learning and mastering the art of Karate. Regular training sessions are conducted, providing members with a platform to refine their Karate techniques, practice various forms, and develop essential self-defense skills. In addition to routine training, the Karate Club actively participates in tournaments, exhibitions, and demonstrations, showcasing the impressive skills honed by its members. The club's presence contributes to a vibrant and energetic atmosphere within the school, fostering a sense of discipline, camaraderie, and physical fitness among its participants.

Houses

The Army Public School Cannanore is organized into four Houses, each named after ancient South Indian dynasties and represented by distinct colors: Chera (Red), Chola (Yellow), Pandya (Green), and Pallava (Blue). These Houses serve as close-knit communities fostering holistic student development. Led by dedicated faculty members, Throughout the academic year, inter-house competitions in sports, academics, arts, and culture provide a platform for students to showcase talents, collaborate, and develop essential life skills, instilling a sense of healthy competition, identity, and camaraderie among students.
