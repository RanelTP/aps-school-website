Admission Guidelines...

Priority

  1. Children of Serving Army Personnel, DSC personnel, Army war widows, Assam Rifles, and TA (When Embodied)
  2. Children of Ex-Serviceman and Ex-DSC men
  3. Children of Serving Air Force and Naval personnel
  4. Children of Ex-Naval/Air Force personnel
  5. Children of Civilians paid out of Defence Estimate, MES, etc.
  6. Other Children subject to availability of vacancies

Guidelines

  1. Fresh Admission to Army Public School will be through an admission test.
  2. All necessary documents are to be attached with the admission form.
  3. Admission of new students shall be made from April and finalized by July.
  4. Admission test will be conducted on dates notified by the Principal.
  5. Admission without Transfer Certificate is made only up to Class IV.
  6. The Principal's decision will be final.
  7. The student should complete the age of 6 years on 31st March for admission to Class I.

The admission test subjects are:

  1. Classes I to IV - English, Hindi, and Mathematics.
  2. Classes VI to X - English, Hindi, Maths, Science.
  3. Classes X & XII - Only from CBSE Stream.
