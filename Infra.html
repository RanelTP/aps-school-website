Computer Lab

The Computer Lab at Army Public School Cannanore is a dedicated space for computer-related activities and education. It includes computers with peripherals, high-speed internet access, a variety of educational software. The lab has a robust networking infrastructure, security features, and individual workstations for students. Provisions for technical support are available.

ATL Lab

The ATL Lab at Army Public School Cannanore is an innovative space dedicated to hands-on learning and fostering creativity among students. It's an Atal Tinkering Lab, empowering young minds to address societal problems through practical projects. Equipped with cutting-edge tools like electronics components and a 3D printer. it encourages active participation, problem-solving, and an entrepreneurial mindset. This lab aligns with India's Atal Innovation Mission, promoting innovation and practical skills while supplementing the regular curriculum. Overall, it's a platform for exploration, innovation, and creative problem-solving, shaping a brighter future for students.

Science Laboratories

The science laboratories at Army Public School Cannanore are designed to offer practical experiences in physics, chemistry, and biology. These well-equipped labs enable students to conduct experiments related to magnetism, chemical reactions, and biological studies. Emphasizing safety and proper techniques, the labs foster a hands-on approach, reinforcing theoretical knowledge and promoting a deep understanding of scientific principles. Overall, they play a crucial role in providing a comprehensive and practical education in the sciences, encouraging exploration and experimentation.
